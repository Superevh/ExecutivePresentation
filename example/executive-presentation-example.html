What the Research Shows
  • Speed asymmetryAI-enabled workers complete knowledge tasks 25–40% faster — but only when the workflow is redesigned around AI, not bolted onto legacy processes.
  • The levelling effectPerformance gains from AI are largest for mid-performers, narrowing the gap with top performers — with major implications for talent strategy.
  • Governance firstOrganisations with clear AI governance frameworks deploy 3× faster and with fewer costly rollbacks than those that treat governance as an afterthought.
